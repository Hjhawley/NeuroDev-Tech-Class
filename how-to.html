How to Use This Website

Welcome to our tech class website! This guide will help you understand how to navigate the site and complete your tech certifications.

Navigating Courses

Each course contains a detailed overview, objectives, and usually three units of learning material. Unit assignments includes readings, videos, and activities to help you learn the material.

Completing Assignments

As you complete the course material, you can check the boxes next to each task to mark them as complete. This helps you track your progress through the course.

Each unit usually ends with a test or a project meant to assess your understanding of the material. Expetations will vary depending on student's current abilities, but typically you'll need a score of 90% or higher to pass the unit.

When you complete a unit, let your tech coach know so they can grade your work. Feel free to move on to the next unit in the meantime.

Getting Certified

Upon successfulyl completing of all units and passing the final tests, you will receive a certification from NeuroDev, showcasing your achievement.

If you have any questions, please reach out to your teach coach.
