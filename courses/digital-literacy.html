Digital Literacy

Course Overview

The goal of this entry-level course is to equip students with essential digital literacy skills, important for both personal and professional success in today's digital world. By mastering basic computer and internet skills, students will improve their productivity, safety, and digital communication abilities. Most adults should find this course useful to some degree, regardless of technical proficiency.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the function of basic hardware and software components.
  • Navigate efficiently through an operating system (Windows or macOS).
  • Employ effective internet search strategies.
  • Practice digital communication etiquette.
  • Apply best practices for digital security and privacy.
  • Utilize common productivity tools and cloud storage solutions.

Unit 1: Intro to Computers

Learn the basics of hardware, software, and computer safety.

Unit 2: Navigating Your OS

Become more comfortable with your operating system of choice (Windows or macOS).

Unit 3: Internet Skills

Develop efficient web searching techniques, email etiquette, and online safety.
