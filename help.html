How to Use This Website

Welcome to the NeuroDev Tech Class website! This guide will help you navigate the site and earn your tech certifications.

Getting Started

Students are strongly encouraged to start with the Digital Literacy course. It covers skills that will give you a good foundation for any course moving forward, and it also gives us helpful information about your current abilities.

That said, if you're feeling confident about your tech savviness, you are welcome to skip the readings and take the test right away.

Course materials are password-protected. Ask your tech coach or a mentor if you need the password.

Navigating Courses

Each course typically includes three units of material. Unit assignments include readings, videos, and activities to help you learn.

As you complete each course unit, you can check the boxes next to each task to mark them as complete. This helps you track your progress.

Each unit typically ends with a test or a project to assess your understanding. The exact expectation may vary based on individual skill levels, but a passing score is usually 90% or higher. Tests are open-note, and we are happy to offer any accommodations you might need. Don't stress!

When you finish a unit, let your tech coach know so they can grade your work. You're welcome to continue on to the next unit in the meantime.

Earning Certifications

Upon successfully completing each unit in a course, you'll receive an official NeuroDev certification. Certificates are awarded at tech ceremonies held every few months and represent all of your hard work.

These certifications demonstrate valuable skills that you can showcase on your resume or job applications. Keep your certificates somewhere safe!
